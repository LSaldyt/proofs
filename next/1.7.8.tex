\problem{Negation Truth Tables}

Construct a truth table showing wether the following are equal:

\begin{itemize}
    \item $\neg (A \wedge B)$
    \item $\neg A \wedge \neg B$
    \item $\neg (A \vee B)$
    \item $\neg A \vee \neg B$
\end{itemize}

\solution

\part 

\begin{tabular}{ |r|r|r|r|r|r| }
 \hline
 $A$ & $B$ & $\neg (A \wedge B)$ & $\neg A \wedge \neg B$ & $\neg (A \vee B)$ & $\neg A \vee \neg B$ \\
 T & T & F & F & F & F &
 T & F & T & F & F & T &
 F & T & T & F & F & T &
 F & F & T & T & T & T &
 \hline
\end{tabular}

Columns 3 and 6 are equal, as are columns 4 and 5.
